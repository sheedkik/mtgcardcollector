Welcome!

This is a pretty sweet app used to be able to keep a list of all your Magic the Gathering cards! Tons of cool features such as adding all of your cards to your collection, as well as being able to add all the special collections that MTG releases every 3 months. So if you have duplicate cards but from different collections, you are able to specify that by adding the specific collection to your card!
